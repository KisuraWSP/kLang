# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in this project! Please take a moment to review the following guidelines before interacting with or contributing to this repository.

## 🍴 Forks and Modification
* **Forks are Highly Recommended:** You are entirely free—and actively encouraged—to fork this repository and modify it to suit your needs. Do whatever you want with your own copy!
* **Experimental Nature:** Please note that any changes, updates, and fixes in this repository are considered strictly **experimental** until they are officially published under a formal Release. 

## 🛑 Pull Requests
* **Contact Me First:** Please **do not** open a Pull Request directly on this repository without prior discussion. I will not review or merge any pull requests unless you have contacted me directly beforehand to discuss the changes.
* **How to reach me:** You can get in touch via **Email** `rresearch125@gmail.com` or **Discord** `Please contact me via email i will personally add you to my discord`.

## 💡 Feature Requests
* You are welcome to submit feature requests. 
* Every request will be reviewed, but please keep in mind that features may be declined if they are deemed unnecessary for the project's core scope or if they introduce too much architectural complexity.
